    Feature: Search for a product on Flipkart

1)Scenario: Search for a product
  
  Given I am on the Flipkart website
  When I enter "laptop" in the search bar
  And I click on the search button
  Then I should see a list of products related to "laptop"
  
2)Scenario: Filter search results by brand
  
  Given I am on the Flipkart website
  When I enter "mobile phone" in the search bar
  And I click on the search button
  And I select "Samsung" from the brand filter
  Then I should see a list of Samsung mobile phones
  
3)Scenario: Add a product to the cart
 
  Given I am on the Flipkart website
  When I search for "t-shirt"
  And I click on a t-shirt product
  And I select a size and color
  And I click on the "Add to cart" button
  Then the t-shirt should be added to my cart
  
4)Scenario: Checkout with a product in the cart
 
  Given I have a product in my Flipkart cart
  When I click on the cart icon
  And I click on the "Checkout" button
  And I enter my address and payment information
  And I click on the "Place Order" button
  Then I should see a confirmation message that my order has been placed
  
5)Scenario: View order history
  
  Given I am logged in to my Flipkart account
  When I click on the "My Orders" tab
  Then I should see a list of my previous orders
